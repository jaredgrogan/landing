Dark Mode Color Schemes

Galactic Twilight

Keywords: Space, Twilight, Electric, Mysterious

A deep and mysterious palette evoking the vastness of space. Rich purples and blacks dominate, accented by electric blues and metallic silvers.

Aurora Night

Keywords: Northern Lights, Vibrant, Mystical

Inspired by the northern lights, this scheme uses dark greens and teals with bright green and magenta accents, creating a vibrant, mystical ambiance.

Cyberpunk Neon

Keywords: Futuristic, Edgy, Cityscape

Futuristic and edgy, this scheme blends dark grays with neon pinks, greens, and blues, capturing the essence of a cyberpunk cityscape.

Midnight Ember

Keywords: Warm, Inviting, Cozy

A warm and inviting palette featuring dark browns and deep reds, complemented by vibrant orange and yellow accents for a cozy, ember-lit feel.

Starlit Ocean

Keywords: Tranquil, Brilliant, Ocean

Combining the tranquility of the ocean with the brilliance of the stars, this scheme features dark blues and greens with silver and white accents.

Mystic Forest

Keywords: Enigmatic, Serene, Forest

Enigmatic and serene, this palette uses dark greens and earthy browns with subtle yellow and green accents, reminiscent of a mystical forest.
