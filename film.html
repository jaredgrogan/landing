Kodak Double X 5222/7222 (Black & White Negative Stock)

A gritty, high-contrast black-and-white film stock used in iconic films like Raging Bull.

Year of Release: 1959

Notable Films: Raging Bull, Schindler's List

Best Feature: Deep blacks, rich grain, classic noir look.

Kodak Tri-X 7266 (Black & White Reversal Stock)

Sharp, punchy black-and-white film stock, known for experimental and documentary filmmaking.
